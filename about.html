Who we are

TB Homes Ghana is a registered Real Estate Company specialised in Brokerage and Development. From a small beginning, we have become one of the fastest-growing Real Estate firms in Ghana.

Since our establishment, we have perfected the process and everything it takes to acquire, manage and develop a property. This has seen us provide world-class services to our clients in areas such as property management, property leasing, property sales, property development, and Real Estate consulting.

We also offer marketing services of large pieces of undeveloped land for commercial and residential purposes devoid of litigation.

Although we are based in Ghana, our services have served clients across Africa, America, Europe and beyond. This makes TB Homes the go-to firm for registered, planned and zoned lands at various locations across Ghana, especially Accra – national capital. You get this at an unbeatable cost.

Our team understands the real estate business like the back of their palms hence, are able to work with clients from conception to the very end. Our success formulae is a right combination of skills, valuable data, powerful network, innovation and a deep understanding of the complexities of the Real Estate market.

With hundreds of successfully closed deals; individuals, organisations, investors, be it local, national or international have come to consider us their prefered partner in all Real Estate related transactions. For most of them, we are “the Property Hub” of Ghana.

TB Homes welcomes you to an opportunity to get the best property at the “right location” and “right price.”
